The Mission

After making the change to a healthier and more sustainable life, Paula encountered a problem in the supply of beauty products with natural ingredients that were available in her local market. None demonstrated the quality and presentation that Paula aspired to find in her bathroom cabinet. There were no exotic ingredients to inspire her scents, and worst of all, none of them worked enough to make her switch to a sustainable product worthwhile. That is why we created Nuet.
